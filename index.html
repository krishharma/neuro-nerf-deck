05 / TECHNICAL APPROACH

Why This AI Approach Is Sound

NeRF-Inspired Mapping
3D scene understanding from image data — no pre-built floor plans required.
MAPS THE UNKNOWN
Visual-Inertial Localization
GPS fails indoors and in rubble. Camera + IMU fusion maintains accurate position.
GPS-DENIED READY
Shared Autonomy
Full manual is exhausting. Full autonomy is too risky in uncertain disaster zones.
OPTIMAL HUMAN-AI SPLIT
EEG High-Level Intent Only
Motor imagery decoding for commands like "advance" or "hold" — not fine motor control. ErrP provides safety vetoes.
REALISTIC & FEASIBLE

The human remains in charge of intent and safety — AI handles complexity and motion planning.

Testing & Validation Plan

A phased, simulation-first approach — grounded in feasibility.

Phase Two

Phase Three
Integrated Shared Autonomy
